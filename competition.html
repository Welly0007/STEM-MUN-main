SPARK: STEM-MUN: Student Perspectives and Research Knowledge

We are thrilled to announce the "SPARK: STEM-MUN Student Perspectives and Research Knowledge 2024," the first Egyptian articles competition organized by high schoolers. This mind-blowing event invites young minds to engage in thoughtful writing and contribute their perspectives on some of the most critical issues facing our society today. Don't hesitate to join our fascinating event, and to put your voice to be a part of the change.

Instructions:
Word Limit: 350 - 450
Citation Style: IEEE
AI is strictly prohibited and leads to immediate elimination
We strongly recommend to have a copy of your answer to avoid any data loss
