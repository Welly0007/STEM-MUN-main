About Us

Model United Nations (MUN) is an academic simulation where the world's most pressing issues are debated and solved using the same procedures as the UN. At MUN, one is bound to witness exhilarating debate on a particular agenda in parliamentary form within parliamentary walls.
STEM Diplomacy
Creating something flawless for the first time is challenging, and maintaining that perfection is even harder. However, our determination to offer a new, exceptional experience for STEM MUN remains strong. Our predecessors set a high standard, and STEM MUN 2020 will continue that legacy. Delegates will enjoy a memorable MUN experience filled with fun, not boredom. Our academic committee is dedicated to delivering information smoothly, helping delegates engage in meaningful debates and consider all potential resolutions. We aim to leave a lasting, positive impression on everyone involved.
LETTER FROM THE SECRETARY
STEM Model United Nations (STEM MUN) aims to provide a platform for young diplomats in the STEM fields to understand international issues and policies, debate, and share perspectives with participants from different backgrounds. This atmosphere fosters the improvement of communication and diplomatic skills, critical thinking, and networking.

Conference

Glimpse from the past

The first STEM MUN conference simulated the UN Security Council with its 15 members and 7 additional members engaged in the Yemen civil war. Delegates were trained in foreign policy, UN principles and procedures, and effective debating. Held at Empire Training and Education in 6th of October City, the conference included 22 delegates, STEM October school principal, school counselor, and STEM Maadi deputy. The event showcased high schoolers' diplomacy and professionalism, earning acclaim from all attendees.
